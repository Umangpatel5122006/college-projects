Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Spendly is a personal expense tracking service. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We aim to collect only what we need to provide the service and to be transparent about how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

To create an account and use Spendly, you provide basic information such as your name and email address, along with a password you choose. When you use the service, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you add.

We also automatically receive standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit within Spendly. This information is used to keep the service secure, reliable, and to diagnose issues.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Create and maintain your account.
  • Store and display the expense records you enter.
  • Generate the summaries, charts, and insights shown in the app.
  • Respond to your questions, support requests, and feedback.
  • Detect, prevent, and address security incidents or abuse.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your expense data to train advertising profiles.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers operated by trusted infrastructure providers. Access to your expense data is limited to systems and personnel that require it to operate the service.

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. Despite these measures, no system is perfectly secure. You are encouraged to use a strong, unique password and to keep your login credentials private.

You may export or permanently delete your account data at any time from your account settings. When you delete an expense, it is removed from your view immediately and purged from our active systems within a reasonable backup-retention window.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of third-party providers to operate the service. These include hosting and database providers that store our application data, and an email service we use to send account notifications and password resets. Each provider receives only the data needed to perform its function and is contractually obligated to protect it.

We do not embed advertising trackers, third-party analytics that follow you across other websites, or social media pixels in Spendly.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You can update your profile details at any time and download a copy of your expense data from your account settings. If you would like to close your account, you may do so from the same area; we will delete your data in line with the retention window described above.

If you have questions about your rights or wish to make a request that is not available through the app, please contact us using the details below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, the service, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the service or by email. Your continued use of Spendly after any changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app.
