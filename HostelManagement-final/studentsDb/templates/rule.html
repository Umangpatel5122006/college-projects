SVNIT Hostel Rules & Regulations

General Conduct

  • Hostel stay is for the academic year unless expelled or withdrawn.
  • No misbehavior or disorderly conduct during events and gatherings.
  • Sexual harassment and privacy violations are strictly banned.
  • Students must not engage in political activities within the campus.
  • Unauthorized absenteeism from rooms is not allowed; strict action follows.
  • Keep noise levels low, especially during silence hours: 10 PM to 6 AM.
  • Rules are subject to revision and will be updated via hostel notice boards.

Visitors & Identity

  • Male visitors are not allowed in girls’ hostels and vice versa without permission.
  • Visitors must leave the hostel by 8 PM; no overnight stays allowed.
  • Hostel ID must be shown on request.
  • Visiting hours: 12 PM–2 PM and 4 PM–7 PM. Entry must be logged in register.
  • Unauthorized persons/services are not allowed inside hostels, especially girls’ hostels.
  • Gate pass is mandatory for late night or emergency movement.

Safety & Security

  • Room inspections by authorities can happen anytime without prior notice.
  • Students are responsible for the safety of their belongings.
  • Fire hazards like candles, incense sticks, and crackers are strictly prohibited.
  • Switch off fans, lights, and electrical devices when not in use.
  • Unauthorized use of fire extinguishers incurs heavy fines.

Disciplinary Actions

  • Any violation of rules may result in warnings, fines, suspension, or expulsion.
  • Repeat violations may result in loss of hostel privileges or degree recommendations.
  • Disciplinary action includes banning from placements or financial aid.

Room & Property

  • Students must stay in their allotted rooms and must not switch without permission.
  • Partying or celebrations in hostel rooms or premises without permission are not allowed.
  • Unauthorized meetings in hostel areas are prohibited.
  • Damage to property leads to fines or recovery costs.
  • All complaints must be logged in the complaint register.

Prohibited Items

  • Ragging is banned and will result in expulsion and police action.
  • No obscene literature, videos, or media possession is allowed.
  • Alcohol, drugs, smoking, tobacco, or any intoxicants are strictly prohibited.
  • Possession or consumption of narcotic substances leads to expulsion and police action.
  • Smoking is banned inside hostel buildings and common rooms.
  • Students must not harbor outsiders or unauthorized individuals.
  • Unauthorized electrical appliances like heaters, irons, etc., are not allowed.

Mess Rules

  • Mess participation is compulsory unless exempted due to health or official leave.
  • Mess menu is created by HMC in consultation with the mess manager.
  • Mess cut is applicable with prior permission for valid reasons only.
  • Food wastage and mess indiscipline are penalized.
  • Suggestions for mess must be made in the suggestion book.

Health & Hygiene

  • Medical issues must be reported to warden or institute doctor immediately.
  • Follow hygiene and cleanliness guidelines at all times.
  • Personal belongings should not be kept in common areas.

Special Conditions

  • Girls must follow extra safety rules, including local guardian visit procedures.
  • Leave form (Annexure 2) must be filled for any travel outside Surat.
  • No student may organize or attend picnics/excursions outside campus without permission.
  • All first-year students must return to the hostel by 7:30 PM every day.
  • Out-of-bounds and opposite-sex entry to hostels is strictly banned.

Anti-Ragging

  • Anti-ragging affidavit must be signed and submitted by all students.
  • Non-reporting of ragging incidents will also be punished.
